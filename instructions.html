How to Play MathsFun-Connect4

Welcome to MathsFun-Connect4! Here's how to play:

  1. Players take turns choosing a column to drop their "pieces" into the Connect 4 grid.
  2. Before placing a piece, you'll need to solve a multiplication problem correctly.
  3. The first player to align four pieces in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally) wins!
